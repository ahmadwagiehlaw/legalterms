تعريف القانون (Law) صفحة 3

"Law is a set of general, abstract, and binding rules that states enact or order their application, accompanied by a present and material penalty imposed by the public authority when these rules are violated."

"القانون هو عبارة عن مجموعة من القواعد العامة والمجردة والملزمة التي تنص الدولة على سنها أو تأمر بتطبيقها، مصحوبة بعقوبة حالية ومادية تفرضها السلطة العامة عند انتهاك هذه القواعد."

تعريف العقد (Contract) صفحة 16

"A contract is one of the most important sources of obligation, and it is an agreement between two wills to produce a legal effect that establishes rights and obligations between the parties of the contract. A contract is concluded by an offer from the first party which is accepted by the second party."

تعريف التحكيم (Arbitration) صفحة 19

"Arbitration is a procedure in which a dispute is submitted, by agreement of the parties, to one or more arbitrators who make a binding decision on the dispute. In choosing arbitration, the parties opt for a private dispute resolution instead of court litigation."
